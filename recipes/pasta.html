Pasta Recipe

Pasta is a staple food, typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, formed into various shapes, and then cooked by boiling or baking.

Ingredients

  • flour(semolina)
  • pasta of your choice
  • olive oil
  • sausages
  • basil
  • salt, pepper
  • cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. Heat the butter and oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat until foaming.
  2. Squeeze large chunks of the sausagemeat out of the skins and into the pan. Cook for 5-8 mins or until golden brown
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook for a further 5 mins until starting to turn brown.
  4. Stir through the paprika and cook for 1 min before stirring in the soured cream, mustard and stock.
  5. Bring to a simmer and season to taste.
